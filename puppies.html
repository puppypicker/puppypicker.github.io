Should I Get A Puppy?

001/006

Do You Really Want A Puppy?

puppies aren't always the cute little fur-balls you want them to be. They need extreme care and dedication.

002/006

Do The People You Live With Want a Dog?

Make sure the people you live with are okay with puppy care because, inevitably, they will be called upon to help out with your dog.

003/006

Does Your Lease Allow You to have pets?

If it does your good, but, if it doesn't speak with your landlord, don't try to sneak it in your house.

004/006

Can You Afford a Dog?

Puppies get sick and accidents happen that you can’t predict. You don’t want to end up in a situation in which you have to choose between caring for your dog and paying your rent.

005/006

Do You Have The Time For Your Puppy?

Puppies require a lot of attention and training. Would your schedule align with your puppies needs?

006/006

Are You Allergic?

You won’t be happy having a dog if you’re allergic. Although there is no such thing as a truly “hypoallergenic” dog, there are some breeds that are less allergy-causing than others
